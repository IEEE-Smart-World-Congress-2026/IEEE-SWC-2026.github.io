# IEEE Smart World Congress 2026 Website

Official website for the **IEEE Smart World Congress 2026**, hosted at the University of Calabria in Rende, Calabria, Italy.

## About the Conference

The IEEE Smart World Congress 2026 is a premier interdisciplinary event that brings together thought leaders, researchers, and practitioners in smart technologies. The conference focuses on the convergence of IoT, AI, smart cities, cybersecurity, and advanced computing, with an emphasis on trustworthy and socially responsible innovation.

**Theme:** *"Convergence of Minds: Forging Human-AI Partnerships Across Disciplines"*

**Location:** Rende, Calabria, Italy  
**Venue:** "Beniamino Andreatta" Convention Center, University of Calabria  
**Dates:** 7-11 September 2026
